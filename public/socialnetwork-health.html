Social Network Health

A working group studying social-network health and applying it to distributed communities. The materials below are organized for developers and researchers who want to dig in.

Software

Personal Network Toolkit

active

A pattern set for building Personal Network Applications (PNAs) — user-owned apps that manage local relationship data outside of SaaS systems. PNAs operate on private local data and on data you choose to share, with strong contracts so users understand and control both their applications and their data.

github.com/richbodo/personal_network_toolkit reference designs: fellows_local_db (directory archive PNA) · PRT (archived; basis for a forthcoming PRM)

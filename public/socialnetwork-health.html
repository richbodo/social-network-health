Personal Network Toolkit

active

A pattern set for building Personal Network Applications (PNAs) — user-owned apps that manage local relationship data outside of SaaS systems. PNAs operate on private local data and on data you choose to share, with strong contracts so users understand and control both their applications and their data.

github.com/richbodo/personal_network_toolkit

  • Reference Designs — fellows_local_db (directory archive PNA) and PRM (personal relationship manager)

SNH Notification Protocol

design note

Connection requests with staged disclosure — a help-seeking protocol that finds a willing, topical, available person across your own contacts or whole communities, revealing as little as possible to as few as possible at every step, with pre-generated fallbacks when no one is available.
